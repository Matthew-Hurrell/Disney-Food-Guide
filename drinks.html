Best Disney Drinks 2022

Fuzzy Tauntaun

Oga's Cantina, Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge, Disney's Hollywood Studios

$17.00

Alcoholic

This unique beverage is an absolute must try for those not afraid of trying something a bit more adventurous! A fuzzy tauntaun is made with cîroc peach vodka, bols peach schnapps, simply orange with tangerine, pure cane sugar, and “buzz button tingling" foam. Believe us, they really aren’t kidding with the tingling foam part! Don’t worry, total mouth numbness is a common side effect!

Stargarita

Space220 Lounge, World Discovery, EPCOT

$16.00

Alcoholic

If you can’t get a reservation for dinner at Space220 why not pop into the Space220 lounge for a signature beverage? The stargarita is made from espolon blanco, cointreau, house made agave sour and b’lure. If you’re a fan of a margarita, you won’t want to miss this epic futuristic twist on the classic drink.
