Best Disney Drinks 2022

Fuzzy Tauntaun

Oga's Cantina, Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge, Disney's Hollywood Studios

$17.00

Alcoholic

This unique beverage is an absolute must try for those not afraid of trying something a bit more adventurous! A fuzzy tauntaun is made with cîroc peach vodka, bols peach schnapps, simply orange with tangerine, pure cane sugar, and “buzz button tingling" foam. Believe us, they really aren’t kidding with the tingling foam part! Don’t worry, total mouth numbness is a common side effect!

Stargarita

Space220 Lounge, World Discovery, EPCOT

$16.00

Alcoholic

If you can’t get a reservation for dinner at Space220 why not pop into the Space220 lounge for a signature beverage? The stargarita is made from espolon blanco, cointreau, house made agave sour and b’lure. If you’re a fan of a margarita, you won’t want to miss this epic futuristic twist on the classic drink.

Tropical Serenade

Aloha Isle, Adventureland, Magic Kingdom Park

$6.99

Non-Alcoholic

Here we have a drink the whole family can enjoy - the Disney classic dole whip float! Disney pros know there is nothing better to quench your thirst on a hot day than a dole whip float. The tropical serenade is made from pineapple-orange-guava juice, coconut soft-serve and an upside-down pineapple cake pop. It’s our favourite sweet refreshing treat.

The Original Peanut Butter Cold Brew

Gideon's Bakehouse, The Landing, Disney Springs

$6.00

Non-Alcoholic

Here’s one for the coffee lovers! The peanut butter cold brew is made with oat milk cold brew, dark roasted peanut blend and maple syrup. It’s the perfect blend of that coffee taste with a little sweetness added in. There’s no better way to get your coffee kick in the morning than with one of these signature drinks! It’s also fully vegan and gluten free!

International Soda Samples

Club Cool, World Celebration, EPCOT

Free

Non-Alcoholic

Here is a nice little pro tip for anyone who’s looking for some cheap refreshments in Epcot. Head on down to Club Cool and sample their free soda samples from around the world! Eight different international flavours are showcased at a time and they change around regularly. Some of the flavours can be a little unique but it’s free so it’s definitely worth checking out!

Frozen Viking Coffee

Kringla Bakeri Og Kafe, World Showcase, EPCOT

$14.00

Alcoholic

We simply couldn’t make this list without including another coffee beverage to appease the caffeine addicts out there! The good news is - this one is alcoholic! Frozen viking coffee is made with baileys Irish cream and kamora coffee liqueur with coffee-chocolate sauce and garnished with coffee-chocolate crunch. Coffee, chocolate and alcohol?! What more could you ask for?

Night Blossom

Pongu Pongu, Pandora, Disney's Animal Kingdom Theme Park

$6.29

Non-Alcoholic

This non-alcoholic beverage is as colourful as it is tasty! The night blossom is comprised of layers of apple and desert pear limeade topped with passionfruit boba balls. A great option for kids! Go and grab one at Pandora - The World of Avatar over at Disney's Animal Kingdom.

Heart of Polynesia

Trader Sam's Grog Grotto, Disney's Polynesian Village Resort

$15.00

Alcoholic

This is a secret menu item that we just had to share with you all! Head on over to trader Sam’s grog grotto for this secret beverage. The heart of Polynesia is a French Polynesian inspired alcoholic cocktail which is made from coruba, bacardi, blue curacao, cream of banana, orange and pineapple. It’s trader Sam’s best kept secret!

Ngumu Jungle Juice

Dawa Bar, Africa, Disney's Animal Kingdom Theme Park

$15.00

Alcoholic

Now it may not look quite as enchanting as some of the other drinks on this menu, but don’t let that fool you. Ngumu jungle juice is a favourite amongst Disney veterans who know what they are drinking! This drink comprises of snow leopard vodka, bols peach schnapps and tusker’s famous jungle juice. It’s a must try for fans of vodka cocktails.

The Orange Brrr'd

Main Street Confectionary, Main Street USA, Magic Kingdom Park

$5.99

Non-Alcoholic

Finally a drink exclusively for kids! Well.. and adventurous adults. This colourful beverage consists of fanta orange, vanilla syrup and lime, all topped off with whipped cream and gummy worms! Sweet is an understatement but hey it’s Disney! Grab one at Main Street Confectionary on Main Street USA in Magic Kingdom.
