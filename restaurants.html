Best Disney Restaurants 2022

'Ohana

Disney's Polynesian Village Resort

Polynesian, American, Family Style, Unique/Themed Dining

$$ - $$$

One of Disney’s most iconic restaurants. This tropical hideaway is truly a must see! If you only have time for one restaurant reservation, this should be the one. Available for breakfast and dinner all you can eat. Great viewing point for the Disney Fireworks Spectacular at Magic Kingdom. The ‘Ohana Bread Pudding is highly recommended!

Be Our Guest

Fantasyland, Magic Kingdom Park

American, French, Unique/Themed Dining

$$$$

Enter the Beast’s enchanted castle for an unforgettable dining experience! Available for lunch and dinner with a three-course French inspired menu. Reservations highly recommended. Try the Grey Stuff, it’s delicious.

Space220

World Discovery, EPCOT

Unique/Themed Dining

$$$ - $$$$

Dine among the stars at Disney’s latest out of this world restaurant! Two or three course meals available for lunch and dinner. Unique space themed dishes combine with the awe-inspiring celestial panorama to create a truly one of a kind experience.

The Hollywood Brown Derby

Hollywood Boulevard, Disney's Hollywood Studios

American, Fine/Signature Dining

$$$

Step into the Golden Age of Hollywood at this authentic replica of the famous Brown Derby, a Tinsletown landmark steeped in glamour and glitz. Contemporary American cuisine with signature dishes and classic cocktails. Available for lunch and dinner. The Cobb Salad is a must try.

California Grill

Disney's Contemporary Resort

American, Seafood, Sushi, Fine/Signature Dining

$$$$

A guest favourite. Savor the finest in Californian cooking as you take in the sights of Seven Seas Lagoon and Magic Kingdom Park. California Grill offers one of the best firework dining experiences at Disney World. Available for dinner with a special limited time 50th Anniversary three course celebration menu.

Cinderella's Royal Table

Fantasyland, Magic Kingdom Park

American, Fine/Signature Dining

$$$ - $$$$

For all the princesses out there - dine inside Cinderella’s castle in this one of a kind fairytale dining experience. Available for a two course set menu for breakfast, or a three course menu for lunch and dinner. This is truly a feast fit for royalty.

Le Cellier Steakhouse

World Showcase, EPCOT

American, Steakhouse, Fine/Signature Dining

$$$

Descend into the cozy cellar of a Canadian chateau for exquisite steaks and seafood specialities. Savor tender steaks, fresh seafood, cheesy poutine fries and other regional cuisine inspired by the provinces of Canada. A steak lovers dream! Available for lunch and dinner.

Story Book Dining at Artist Point

Disney's Wilderness Lodge

American, Character Dining

$$$$

A delicious dinner the whole family can enjoy! With special appearances from Disney characters from the Disney film Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs. Enter the enchanted forest to the sounds of delightful music that help bring that storybook fantasy to life. Heigh-Ho, Heigh-Ho - It’s Off to Dinner We Go!

Topolino's Terrace

Disney's Riviera Resort

French, Italian, Character Dining, Fine/Signature Dining

$$$

Topolino’s Terrace offers delicious French and Italian cuisine, along with sweeping rooftop views over Disney’s Caribbean Beach Resort. Available for breakfast and dinner. If you are a fan of breakfast, this is a must see. One of the best character breakfasts in the whole of Disney World!

Chef Art Smith's Homecomin'

The Landing, Disney Springs

American, Casual Dining

$$

Dig into farm-to-fork cruise showcasing Florida’s freshest flavours - all crafted from recipes by this award-winning celebrity chef! Open for brunch, lunch and dinner. This is American comfort food at it’s finest. Enjoy the welcoming vibe at this down-home kitchen and shine bar. That’s some good eatin’!
