Best Disney Snacks 2022

Cheshire Cat Tail

Cheshire Cafe, Fantasyland, Magic Kingdom Park

$5.79

The Cheshire Cat Tail is an iconic Disney snack. This delicious treat is a flakey pastry filled with melted chocolate and topped with drizzled purple and blue icing. It’s an absolute must try for anyone looking for a sweet snack while in Magic Kingdom! Treat yourself!

Carrot Cake Cookie

Trolley Car Cafe, Hollywood Boulevard, Disney's Hollywood Studios

$4.99

Big eaters ahoy! The beloved carrot cake cookie has officially returned to Disney’s Hollywood Studios after an extended hiatus! This utterly delicious snack is made with yummy cream cheese frosting sandwiched between two cake-like carrot cake cookies. I hope you’re hungry, because this truly is a feast! Perfect to share or snack on all day!

The Original Chocolate Chip Cookie

Gideon's Bakehouse, The Landing, Disney Springs

$6.00

What can we say? Just look at that photo. This one happens to be a favourite of our team here at Disney Food Guide. These cookies have been rated as some of the best in the world. They are an absolute must try. Pure cookie magic. Pro tip - hot cookie hour is 7 to 8pm. Thank us later.

Ronto Wrap

Ronto Roasters, Galaxy's Edge, Disney's Hollywood Studios

$12.99

Any Star Wars fans out there? Here’s something juicy to sink your teeth into. The famous Ronto Wrap at Ronto Roasters. A savoury grilled sausage with sliced roasted pork, topped with a slightly spicy peppercorn sauce and a tangy slaw all wrapped in a pitta. It’s the perfect snack if you feel in the mood for something savoury.

Pineapple Dole Whip Float with Tiki Sipper

Aloha Isle, Adventureland, Magic Kingdom Park

$11.99

Looking for something a little more refreshing? This is the ideal snack if you’re looking to take a break to cool down on a hot day. Dole whip is a Disney World favourite for many guests, and this one comes with a funky souvenir tiki sipper cup! What’s not to love?

50th Celebration Glimmer and Shimmer Blondie

Fairfax Fare, Sunset Boulevard, Disney's Hollywood Studios

$4.49

This is one of the most reasonably priced options on this list! But don’t let that put you off, this is one tasty treat! Gooey toffee and toasted coconut blondie with salted caramel buttercream and festive gold nuggets all topped off with a chocolate 50th anniversary button. It’s certainly sweet, but that’s definitely not a bad thing!

50th Celebration Petit Cake

Amorette's Patisserie, Town Center, Disney Springs

$22.00

We have another special 50th Celebration limited time snack for you now! This is one you won’t want to miss! The Petit Cake is a vanilla chiffon cake with celebration blue mousse, blueberry jam and lemon curd. This is probably one of the most beautiful cakes we’ve ever seen, as I’m sure you’ll agree! It almost looks too good to eat… Almost.

Baton Rouge Beignets

Scat Cat's Club Cafe, Disney's Port Orleans Resort - French Quarter

$10.99

Who says you can’t get drunk while eating? That’s the magic of Disney, baby! These three boozy beignets are jazzed up with your choice of baileys irish cream liqueur, kahlúa liqueur or rumchata liqueur. The infusion takes place with the help of pipettes making the most of the hollow beignets. Just don’t eat too many or you may have trouble leaving!

Kitchen Sink

Beaches & Cream Soda Shop, Disney's Beach Club Resort

$35.00

I hope you’re hungry, because this one is truly a monster! The kitchen sink comprises of scoops of vanilla, chocolate, strawberry, cookies ‘n cream, and mint chocolate chip topped with hot fudge, peanut butter, snickers pieces, fresh-baked brownie, and smothered in every additional topping they have. Technically this has been advertised as a meal for four, but we’re going to leave that up to you!

Casey's Corn Dog Nuggets

Casey's Corner, Main Street USA, Magic Kingdom Park

$10.29

Now for our final entry. No Disney snack list would be complete without Casey’s corn dog nuggets! These little nuggets are crispy and delicious and come with an option of French fries or a cuties mandarin. Just make sure you get a side of cheese sauce for $1.00 to dip them in! Yummy!
